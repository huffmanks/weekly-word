Fighting the wrong battles

James 4

Matt Humphrey

Explores the root of conflict, the dangers of pride and the futility of self-centered plans, urging humility and dependence on God’s direction.

The Book of James
Verses
  • James 4
  • 1 John 2:15-16
  • Philippians 2:3-11
  • Luke 22:14-30
  1. We often fight with others 🤼‍♀️ because we are fighting 🥊 within.
  2. When our focus 🔎 is fighting for ourselves, pride 🏋️ causes us to be opposed to the Lord.
  3. Fighting for our own plans 🗓️ is like navigating with a broken compass 🧭.
